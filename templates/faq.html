Frequently Asked Questions

For Job Seekers

How do I apply for a job?

Click on any job to view details, then follow the application instructions provided by the employer.

Do I need an account to apply?

No, you can view jobs without an account. However, employers may require you to contact them directly.

For Employers

How do I post a job?

Register as an employer, verify your email, and click "Post Job" in your dashboard.

How long does approval take?

Jobs are typically approved within 24-48 hours by our admin team.

Can I upload images?

Yes! You can upload industry photos that will appear in the community feed.

General

Is this platform free?

Yes, posting and viewing jobs is completely free. We may introduce premium features in the future.

How do I contact support?

Visit our Contact page to send us a message.
